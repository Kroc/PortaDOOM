Title                   : 3 Heures d'agonie 2
Filename                : 3ha2.wad
Release date            : November, the 30th 2014
Authors                 : 3ha2 Team
Email Address           : jcdoom@hotmail.fr
General Description     : 3 Heures d'agonie 2 is a collection of 32 vanilla
			speedmaps designed by members of the French Doom Community. 
			The general rule was that each map layout was to be made 
			within a time limit of 3 hours.
