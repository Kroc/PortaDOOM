* Construction *

Base                    : New from scratch
Build Time              : Started 27th of April, 2014
Editor(s) used          : Doom Builder, SLADE3, WhackEd4
Known Bugs              : -Laying multiple Jackbombs at once can 
                          occasionally cause some of them to disappear in
                          PrBoom+
                          -The sprites in ZDoom's alternate HUD are
                          somewhat jumbled due to various bits of
                          DeHackEd tomfoolery
                          -There is a small chance of encountering stuck 
                          monsters in the final fight in ports that don't 
                          enforce monster spawner telefragging: I've done 
                          what I can to minimize the chance of it 
                          happening, but it's still possible
May Not Run With        : Vanilla Doom
Tested With             : PrBoom+, ZDoom, GZDoom
