* informations *

- look in every room/area before entering a key-door
- walking on nukage does not decrease your health, 
  but the radiation areas do it, they are signed
- you can lower the red cylinders
- if you don't know how to get the blue key, look above
  or use the computer map
- Difficulty Settings: skill 2 (for a smooth playing) and 
  skill 4 (not very hard) have a similar monster-setting
  skill 3 (first built) has a different monster- and thing-setting
- first planed to make one map, now you have two


* story (if you like) *

He recieved an SOS from his two best friends, which said that they were
trying to escape with their spaceship from the mars-base, who they were
working for the UAC Company. Asking them, what happend, the connection 
interrupted. He went to the mars to help his friends and (game-start) 
finds the space-ship left. Suddenly he is beamed - the first time in 
his live. Everyone is angry to him. Will he find his friends? 
We wish him good luck.
