Title                   : No End in Sight
Filename                : NEIS.WAD
Release date            : 11/28/16
Version                 : 1.0
Author                  : Emil "NaturalTvventy" Brundage
                          Xaser Acheron
                          Chris Lutz
Email Address           : [redacted]

Description             : Oldschool 4-episode adventure for The Ultimate
                          Doom, featuring some of the most devilish map
                          design this side of 1994. 'Nuff said... ish.

                          Similar to DTWID, the spirit of id is ever-present,
                          albeit cranked up to 11 with a devious new spin
                          on the classic episodes' themes. Along the way,
                          you'll enter parallel worlds, explore derelict
                          spaceships still floating in the sky, and return
                          to some eerily familiar locales on the far side
                          of Phobos and beyond.

Story or something      : With no end in sight(tm) to the demon presence
                          on Earth, our hero, Doomguy McDoomperson, decides
                          to retrace his steps through Phobos, Deimos, and
                          Hell in order to discover a permanent solution to
                          a certain damnatious pest problem... and also
                          because he was bored.

Additional Credits to   : Plenty of folks! See "Full Credits" below.

Base                    : New from scratch

May Not Run With...     : Versions of Doom prior to 1.9.
