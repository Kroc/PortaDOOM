* Play Information *

Game                    : DooM2

Map #                   : 1 - 32

Single Player           : Of course

Cooperative 2-4 Player  : Yes, but not tested.

Deathmatch 2-4 Player   : Yes, but not tested. Many levels are altered
			  in-game to make them more hospitable to DM play.

Difficulty Settings     : Yes, so I don't want to hear any whining about it
			  being too difficult.


* Construction *

Base                    : New Levels

Build Time              : 4+ Years

Known Bugs              : Savegame Buffer Overflow on many of the later
			  levels. Use a port if you intend to save.

			  Slight possibility of VPO on MAP07, MAP08, MAP26.

			  Occasional HOM. Not severe enough to interfere
			  with gameplay.
